About Polygon
Polygon (also known as Matic Network) is a decentralized platform that is built on top of the Ethereum blockchain. It is designed to provide fast, cheap, and secure transactions for developers building on Ethereum.

About Optimism
Optimism is a fast, stable, and scalable L2 blockchain. Built as a minimal extension to existing Ethereum software, Optimism’s EVM-equivalent architecture scales your Ethereum apps without surprises. If it works on Ethereum, it works on Optimism at a fraction of the cost.
